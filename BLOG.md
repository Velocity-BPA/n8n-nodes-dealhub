# Automate Your Sales Workflow: Introducing the DealHub Community Node for n8n

We're excited to announce that Velocity BPA has created a new community node that brings DealHub's powerful sales automation capabilities directly into your n8n workflows!

## The Challenge: Disconnected Sales Tools

Sales teams juggle multiple platforms daily—CRM systems, proposal tools, product catalogs, and deal rooms. Manually syncing data between DealHub and other business applications creates bottlenecks, introduces errors, and wastes valuable selling time. Your revenue operations deserve better.

## Meet the n8n-nodes-dealhub Solution

Our new DealHub community node bridges this gap by enabling seamless automation between DealHub and hundreds of other applications through n8n. Whether you're managing quotes, synchronizing your product catalog, updating CRM records, or tracking DealRoom activities, this node puts automation at your fingertips.

## Key Features

The DealHub node provides comprehensive access to DealHub's core functionality:

- **Quote Management**: Create, update, and retrieve quotes automatically based on triggers from your CRM or other business systems
- **DealRoom Integration**: Monitor deal progress, update stakeholders, and trigger actions based on buyer engagement
- **Product Catalog Sync**: Keep your product information consistent across all platforms
- **CRM Synchronization**: Bidirectional data flow between DealHub and your CRM system

## Getting Started

Installation is simple. In your n8n instance, navigate to Settings > Community Nodes and search for "dealhub", or install directly via npm:


npm install n8n-nodes-dealhub


Once installed, you'll find the DealHub node in your n8n node palette, ready to connect with your existing workflows.
